Privacy Policy

How We Collect, Use, and Protect Guest Information

Capeway Inn & Suites respects the privacy of guests, website visitors, and people who contact us about a stay. We collect only the information reasonably needed to respond to inquiries, process reservation requests, provide hotel services, communicate with guests, and operate our website.

Information We May Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address, mailing address, and other contact details.
  • Reservation details, including check-in and check-out dates, room preference, guest count, special requests, and stay history.
  • Payment, deposit, billing, or transaction information when required to confirm or complete a booking.
  • Messages, forms, calls, emails, or other communications sent to the hotel.
  • Basic website information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, referral source, and cookie preferences.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to booking inquiries and confirm availability, pricing, and reservation details.
  • To process deposits, payments, cancellations, refunds, invoices, and receipts.
  • To prepare for and provide guest stays, including room assignment and guest service requests.
  • To send important service messages about a reservation, policy update, payment, or stay.
  • To improve our website, booking process, guest communications, and hotel operations.
  • To meet legal, tax, accounting, security, fraud prevention, and regulatory obligations.

Storage and Protection

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical steps to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Information is kept only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers to help operate the website and hotel business, including email, payment processing, booking tools, analytics, hosting, security, forms, and customer communication services. These providers may process information only as needed to perform services for us and are expected to protect personal information appropriately.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies for required site functions, preference storage, performance measurement, analytics, and booking experience improvements. Guests may manage browser cookie settings at any time. Some features may not work properly if required cookies are blocked.

Privacy Rights

Guests may contact us to ask about their personal information, request corrections, or raise privacy questions. Where applicable, we handle personal information in accordance with Canadian privacy requirements and the laws of Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada.

Terms and Conditions

Website Use and Guest Responsibilities

By using this website, submitting a reservation request, contacting the hotel, or staying with us, guests agree to use our services respectfully and provide accurate information. These terms are intended to protect guests, staff, hotel property, and the booking process.

Website and Reservation Requests

  • Guests must provide complete and accurate contact, stay, and payment information.
  • Guests must not submit false, misleading, fraudulent, or unauthorized reservation requests.
  • Website content, rates, photos, descriptions, and availability may change without notice.
  • Errors may occur. If a listed rate, availability result, or description is incorrect, the hotel may correct the error before confirming a booking.

Request-to-Book and Availability

A submitted booking form or inquiry is not always a confirmed reservation. If our system is operating on a request-to-book basis, reservations are subject to room availability, price confirmation, payment or deposit requirements, and hotel approval. A booking is confirmed only when the hotel sends confirmation by email, phone, message, or other accepted method.

Check-In and Check-Out

  • Standard check-in time is 3:00 p.m., unless the hotel confirms another time.
  • Standard check-out time is 11:00 a.m., unless the hotel confirms another time.
  • Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may involve additional charges.
  • Guests may be asked to provide valid identification and a valid payment method at check-in.

House Rules

  • Smoking or vaping is not permitted in non-smoking rooms or restricted indoor areas. Cleaning or damage fees may apply.
  • Pets are permitted only if approved by the hotel in advance. Pet rules, restrictions, or fees may apply.
  • Guests are responsible for damage, excessive cleaning, missing items, or loss caused by themselves or their visitors.
  • Guests must respect quiet hours, staff directions, other guests, occupancy limits, and local laws.
  • Parties, disruptive conduct, unsafe behaviour, or unlawful activity may result in charges, removal, or cancellation without refund.
  • The number of guests in a room must not exceed the confirmed occupancy limit.

Limitation of Responsibility

The hotel is not responsible for interruptions or events outside its reasonable control, including weather, power outages, travel delays, third-party service issues, or emergency conditions. Guests are responsible for their personal belongings and should secure valuables.

Governing Law

These terms and all hotel policies are governed by the laws of Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada, and applicable Canadian laws.

Booking / Cancellation / Refund Policy

Reservations, Payments, Cancellations, and Refunds

We want booking details to be clear before arrival. Specific terms may vary by room type, season, package, group booking, third-party booking channel, or written confirmation from the hotel. If your confirmation states different terms, the confirmed booking terms will apply.

Deposits and Payment

  • A deposit, credit card hold, or full payment may be required to confirm a reservation.
  • Payment requirements will be provided before or at the time the hotel confirms the booking.
  • Guests are responsible for room charges, taxes, approved add-ons, extra guest charges, damages, and incidental fees.
  • Rates are subject to applicable taxes and may vary by date, room type, availability, and length of stay.

Cancellations and Changes

  • Guests should contact the hotel as soon as possible to cancel or change a reservation.
  • Unless stated otherwise in the booking confirmation, cancellations should be made at least 48 hours before the scheduled check-in date.
  • Late cancellations may be charged a fee up to the cost of one night's stay, plus applicable taxes.
  • Changes to dates, room type, guest count, or length of stay are subject to availability and current rates.

No-Shows

If a guest does not arrive on the scheduled check-in date and does not cancel within the required cancellation period, the reservation may be treated as a no-show. No-shows may be charged up to the cost of one night's stay, plus applicable taxes, or another amount stated in the booking confirmation.

Refund Eligibility

  • Eligible refunds depend on the confirmed rate, cancellation timing, payment method, and any special terms accepted at booking.
  • Refunds are generally available for cancellations made before the applicable cancellation deadline, less any stated non-refundable fees.
  • Non-refundable rates, promotional offers, group bookings, extended stays, or special event bookings may have stricter terms.
  • Refunds may not be available for late cancellations, no-shows, early departures, or policy violations.

Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are normally processed within 5 to 10 business days after approval. Banks, payment processors, credit card networks, or third-party booking platforms may take additional time to post the refund to the guest's account.

Extra Charges

  • Extra guest charges may apply when the number of guests exceeds the standard occupancy for the booked room.
  • Additional fees may apply for approved pets, late check-out, excessive cleaning, smoking, damages, missing items, or other special services.
  • The hotel may charge the payment method on file for approved charges, unpaid balances, damage, or fees permitted by these policies.
