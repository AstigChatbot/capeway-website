About Capeway Inn

Restful rooms, real hospitality, and a remarkable coastal setting.

The Capeway Inn is located on Route 100, just minutes from Cape St. Mary's Ecological Reserve, the most accessible seabird colony in North America.

Once a Presentation convent, the building has been thoughtfully renovated into an inn in the heart of scenic St. Bride's, Newfoundland. The Capeway Inn has been operating in its current capacity since 1994.
