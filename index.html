A Modern Welcome

Restful rooms, real hospitality, and a sense of place.

Capeway Inn & Suites is imagined here as a polished coastal retreat with the friendly spirit of a family-run Newfoundland stay. Wake to fresh air, return from day trips to a calm room, and enjoy the simple confidence of thoughtful essentials.

Whether you are crossing the peninsula, visiting family, or lingering for the landscape, the inn keeps the experience grounded: comfortable beds, practical room features, warm service, and a location that invites unhurried exploration.
